Passes and Assists

 This visualization shows the spatial distribution of assists over the pitch. An assist is commonly understood to be the last pass made to the scorer of a goal. The pitch show in the visualization below is a regulation-sized 120-by-80 yard field, divided into a 6x3 grid. Each cell of the grid represents a zone. The team's direction of play is to the right.

 In the 2010s, FC Barcelona were champions of the 'tiki-taka' style of football.

How to read this visualization:
Each assist is denoted by a ring drawn on the pitch.The color intensity of a zone represents the frequency of assists that orginated from that zone.
